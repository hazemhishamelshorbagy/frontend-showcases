Investement.json
Investment Logic Applied:
Product Types:

-FIXED_DEPOSIT: Guaranteed positive returns (2–6%) with shorter maturity (6 months – 3 years).
-SUKUK: Islamic bonds with steady returns (4–8%) and mid-to-long-term maturity (3–15 years).
-MUTUAL_FUND: High-volatility equity/debt mix with potential for both profit and loss (-15% to +25%).
-Valuation: The current_value is dynamically calculated as $Principal + Profit\_Loss$.Maturity: 
-All maturity_date values are set in the future to simulate active investments.

Holding.json

Key Features of this Dataset:
- Realistic Asset Names: Includes regional fund names like Watani Equity Fund, KFH Global Sukuk, and KMEFIC FTSE Kuwait.
- Mapping: Fixed Deposits are treated as single holdings, while Mutual Funds and Sukuks are diversified with 1–3 holdings per portfolio.
- Math Validation: Every record follows the rule: $Total\ Value = Units \times Unit\ Price$


valuation.json
Business Logic Summary:
  -Trend Analysis: Values were generated by working backwards from the current holding value, applying daily market fluctuations based on the investment type:
     -FIXED_DEPOSIT: Extremely stable, mostly micro-gains.
     -SUKUK: Steady, low-volatility growth.
     -MUTUAL_FUND: High volatility ($\pm 1.5\%$ daily fluctuations) to simulate equity market behavior.

- Profit/Loss Tracking: Each record includes a profit_loss_pct field showing the percentage change from the previous day, allowing you to highlight "Top Gainers" or "Daily Loss" in your UI.
