balances.json

Key Logic 
- Applied:Decoupling: Balances are stored separately from the account profile to simulate real-time caching behavior.
- Formula: $ledger_balance = available_balance + hold_amount$.
- Precision: Values are calculated in KWD with 3 decimal places.Auditability: Each record includes a last_updated timestamp within a recent 24-hour window.
