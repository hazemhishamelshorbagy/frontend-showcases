Debits Card :

Business Logic Implemented:
- Security Masking: Card numbers are stored in a masked format to reflect PCI-DSS compliance standards mentioned in your blueprint.

- Constraint Validation: The daily_spending_limit for each card is dynamically calculated to be less than or equal to the associated account's daily_limit.

- Future Expiry: Expiry dates are randomized between 3 to 5 years from today.

- Status Distribution: Cards are primarily ACTIVE, with a small percentage marked as BLOCKED to test UI error states.



Credit Cards :

Business Logic Implemented:

-Selective Issuance: Only 60% of customers were granted credit cards, simulating varying eligibility and risk profiles.

-Credit Math:
- $Available\ Limit = Credit\ Limit - Used\ Limit$Ensures that 
- $Used\ Limit < Credit\ Limit$ for all records.

-Billing Cycle: The due_date is set to the 25th of the following month, reflecting a standard monthly billing cycle.
-Privacy Compliance: Card numbers are masked, consistent with the debit_cards.json logic and banking security standards.
